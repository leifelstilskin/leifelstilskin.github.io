Chapter 2, Price Calculator

What I wanted to do is take the normal calculator and tweak it to serve a purpose I'd need on a site like mine. Obviously a random calculator is a bit odd most times but the basic development seems to be the foundation for creating functions and adding scripts. So I took the normal 1-9 buttons then replaced them with products and services a site might have. Then took the values that would be added in association with them to a comparative amount, (ie 1 tshirt = 25 (amount in dollars)). In the end what I wanted to do was just make it applicable to my site. Which does need some work aestetically but slowly it will get there. Thanks for looking!
