Gone Away To Japan

The Stilskin Team recently went to Japan and ran the streets of Tokyo from the moment they landed in Narita to the moment they flew out three days later.

Hello to anyone reading

Hello, my name is Leif. I am the "creative head" of Stilskin Co. What Stilskin is, is an art collective. From Photography to Tshirts, street article to stickers its about creating for those who strive to forward their craft. This last trip to Tokyo in August was a trip for just that. A few friends of STKN took a trip to Tokyo Japan seeking out new influences and rad street Photography shots on 35mm Cameras. That gallery is soon to come.
